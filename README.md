# BMI Calculator
This BMI calculator calculates your BMI (Body Mass Index) by entering your height and weight. The BMI is meant to give you a general idea of what your weight should be for your height.

### BMI Formula

BMI =
Mass (Kilograms)
/
(Height (Meters))2
